01
Content Format

Double Down on Carousel & Video

Carousels drive a 13.4% avg engagement rate vs 9.0% for images — 49% higher. Reels/Videos achieve 6,400+ avg likes. Shift content mix to 40% Carousel, 35% Video, 25% Image.

02
Timing

Post on Sundays & Fridays

Sunday delivers 12.2% avg engagement — the highest of any weekday. Friday follows at 11.5%. Schedule high-effort posts (new launches, tutorials) on these days for maximum reach.

03
Consistency

Maintain 12–18 Posts Per Month

Peak performance months (Jan, Feb, Jun–Aug) average 15–19 posts. Months with <10 posts show growth stagnation. Build a content calendar to ensure steady cadence.

04
Content Themes

Lean into Festive & Lip Content

Festive Looks and Lip Shade hashtag clusters show highest avg engagement rates. Plan campaign bursts around Diwali (Oct–Nov) and Valentine's Day (Feb) with 3–4 weeks of dedicated content.

05
Growth

Boost Saves with Tutorial Content

Carousels have 2.6× more saves than images, indicating high reference value. Step-by-step makeup tutorials and "Get Ready With Me" carousel posts drive saves, which signal quality to the algorithm.

06
Virality

Optimize for Shares with Bold Launches

Shares (111K total) are the key virality driver. Posts announcing new collections or limited-edition launches get 2–3× average shares. Pair every launch with a shareable comparison reel.
